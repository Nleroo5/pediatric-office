Our Providers

Meet the dedicated physicians and nurse practitioners who lead our practice

Dr. Austin Dupont, MD

Owner & Lead Pediatrician

University of Toledo College of Medicine

Residency: University of Toledo Pediatric Program

Hi! I'm Dr. Austin Dupont, a pediatrician passionate about providing trusted, compassionate, and down-to-earth care to children and their families. I completed medical school and pediatric residency in Toledo, Ohio, before moving to Jacksonville with my wife, Jade, in 2022. After an unforgettable honeymoon backpacking through Europe, we settled down here and welcomed our daughter, Daisy Mae, in the fall of 2024.

In September 2025, I was blessed with the opportunity to purchase Village Pediatrics from Dr. Pat Elvir, who founded the practice in 2007. Dr. Elvir has been both a mentor and an inspiration to me, and I deeply admire the warm, family-centered culture she built. It's an honor to carry on her legacy at this cornerstone practice in the St. Augustine community.

I'm known for being easy-going and fun (expect a high-five or fist bump when I walk in!), but I take my role in your child's health very seriously. I look forward to getting to know your family and walking alongside you through every stage of your child's journey.

Special Interests & Expertise

  • ADHD Diagnosis & Management
  • Anxiety & Depression
  • Asthma Management
  • Constipation Management
  • Healthy Habits (Pediatric Weight Management Program)

Outside the Office

You'll usually find me at church, spending quality time with my family, working on home projects, or out in nature. I love woodworking, archery with my compound bow, and traveling—especially my past solo backpacking adventures through Europe. Fun fact: my family and I rolled up our sleeves and handled many of the office renovations ourselves, including adding a brand-new 4th exam room and fresh paint throughout!

Dr. Patricia Elvir, MD

FAAP, ABIHM

Founding Physician

University of San Carlos, Guatemala

Dr. Elvir is a native New Yorker who completed her pediatric residency at Schneider Children's Hospital, serving as Chief Resident. She is dual board certified with the American Board of Pediatrics and Integrative and Holistic Medicine, with over 20 years of experience.

An ordained minister and founder of Amigos de Juanita, she has a heart for medical missions to Central America and speaks fluent Spanish. She founded Village Pediatrics in 2008 and grew it into a beloved practice serving the St. Augustine area.

Mission Work

Medical missions in Guatemala, Nicaragua, and Haiti

Trudy English, ARNP

Pediatric Nurse Practitioner

University of Akron BSN

Ohio State University MSN

Trudy graduated with her BSN in 1989 and worked in the Neonatal Intensive Care Unit at Ohio State University Medical Center before realizing her passion for pediatrics. She earned her Masters in advanced pediatric nursing in 1996 and has been with Village Pediatrics since 2015.

Trudy and her husband Robert have three children (twins Grace and Will, and daughter Annie). She enjoys caring for her barn animals including two donkeys, three goats, and 18 hens!

Personal Interests

Beach combing, puzzles, reading, and family time

Dr. Lindsay Wolf, DNP

MBA, APRN, CPNP-PC, CNE, CLC

Jacksonville University Professor

University of Florida DNP

Dr. Wolf is a full-time Associate Professor and Associate Dean of Nursing at Jacksonville University, teaching in multiple programs while practicing as an autonomous pediatric nurse practitioner one day per week.

She is certified as a Nurse Educator, Pediatric Nurse Practitioner, and Lactation Counselor. Her main interests include growth and development, children with special needs, and lactation support for breastfeeding families.

Specializations

Lactation support, special needs, growth & development
