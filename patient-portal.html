Getting Started is Easy

Follow these three simple steps to access your patient portal and start managing your healthcare online.

01

Complete Your First Visit

Schedule and complete your initial appointment at Village Pediatrics. Our team will gather your information and medical history.

02

Receive Portal Invitation

Within 24-48 hours after your visit, you'll receive an email invitation to set up your patient portal account.

03

Create Your Account

Click the invitation link in your email and follow the prompts to create your secure portal account with a username and password.
