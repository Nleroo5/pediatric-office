Essential Care Services

Well Child Visits

Comprehensive checkups designed to monitor your child's growth, development, and overall health at every stage, from newborns through age 21.

Same-Day Sick Visits

We will always strive to ensure your children are able to be seen quickly, with same-day sick appointments available daily!

Developmental and Autism Screenings

We follow the American Academy of Pediatrics' recommended screening schedule, designed to ensure your child is meeting their age-appropriate developmental milestones.

Hours Designed to Fit Your Family's Schedule

With extended weekday hours and Saturday weekend hours, Village Pediatrics is there when your family needs us most.

Vaccines & Immunizations

At Village Pediatrics, we strongly believe in the importance and value of childhood immunizations to protect our children, families, and community. We recommend and encourage our patients to follow the routine CDC vaccination schedule, which has been proven safe and effective in preventing serious illness.

At the same time, we understand that every family's journey is unique. We are well known in the St. Augustine community for our welcoming and respectful approach to families including those who choose alternative or delayed vaccine schedules.

It is our promise and pledge that we will never treat you or your child differently based on a decision you make as a parent in regards to vaccines. Our goal is always to ensure open communication built on mutual respect and trust, and to work together as partners centered on promoting your child's health and well-being.

Sports & School Physicals

Need that all-important sport physical or back-to-school form? We have you covered!

Meet the Pediatrician

Looking for a pediatrician in whom you can entrust the care of your precious newborn? Get a tour of the office, meet the staff, and schedule a free Meet-The-Pediatrician appointment today!

Newborn Care

At Village Pediatrics, we prioritize newborn care with exceptional attention and compassion. We do this by dedicating 45 minutes to every first newborn visit so that your trusted pediatric provider can ensure your baby is healthy, thriving, and off to the best possible start. We understand that welcoming a new baby comes with countless questions, and our team takes the time to provide thoughtful guidance, reassurance, and support - for both new parents and newborns!

Pediatric Obesity Management

Through our Healthy Habits initiative, we are proud to offer a comprehensive pediatric weight management program that focuses on the promotion of health-focused lifestyle changes.

Asthma Management

Our pediatric providers are well-trained and confident in diagnosing and managing both acute asthma "attacks" as well as chronic asthma management.

Mental Health Support

Anxiety and Depression Screenings are routinely administered to our adolescent and teenage patients. Our providers are passionate about managing both anxiety and depression, and we have a great referral network of locally trusted and caring mental health specialists.

Telehealth Visits

Convenient virtual appointments from the comfort of your home.

Ear Piercings

Ear piercings available for all children over 2 months old! Through our partnership with Blomdahl, we use nickel-free hypoallergenic medical-grade titanium earrings that come in a variety of beautiful, timeless designs.

Wart Removal

We use in-house cryotherapy to safely and effectively treat those pesky warts.

Village Pediatrics is proud to serve as a medical home for our medically complex children, offering timely care and referral services, with up-to-date and comprehensive care.
