Owner & Lead Pediatrician

Areas of Expertise

ADHD Diagnosis & Management

Asthma Management

Dr. Patricia Elvir, MD

FAAP, ABIHM

Founding Physician

20+ Years Experience

The visionary who founded Village Pediatrics in 2008, Dr. Elvir continues to provide the compassionate care that families have trusted for years. A native New Yorker who served as Chief Resident at Schneider Children's Hospital.

Credentials & Service

Board Certified: American Academy of Pediatrics (FAAP)
Dual Certified: American Board of Integrative & Holistic Medicine (ABIHM)
Languages: Fluent in Spanish
Mission Work: Medical missions to Guatemala, Nicaragua, Haiti
Community: Ordained minister, Founder of Amigos de Juanita

Trudy English, ARNP
