Our Journey

How Village Pediatrics grew into the practice families trust today

2008

The Beginning

Dr. Patricia Elvir founded Village Pediatrics with a vision to serve St. Augustine families with comprehensive, compassionate care. Starting with a commitment to excellence and community service.

2008-2025

Building Trust

Over 17 years, Village Pediatrics grew into a beloved pediatric practice, establishing same-day appointments, 24/7 after-hours nursing, and comprehensive care that families could rely on.

September 2025

New Chapter

Dr. Austin Dupont becomes the new owner, bringing fresh leadership while maintaining the same compassionate care and trusted team that families have come to love.

What Makes Us Different
