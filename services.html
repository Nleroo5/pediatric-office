Our Services

VILLAGE PEDIATRICS

Our Services Include

  • Well-Child Visits
    Routine checkups to monitor growth, development, and overall health. These visits include physical exams, screenings, immunizations, and time to answer your questions.
  • Sick Visits
    Same-day appointments available for sudden illnesses like fever, cough, rashes, stomach concerns, and more.
  • Immunizations
    We follow the CDC-recommended vaccine schedule to keep your child protected. Our team takes the time to explain each vaccine and answer your questions.
  • Newborn Care
    Gentle, supportive care for your newest arrival. We partner with parents to ensure a smooth and healthy transition during your baby's early days.
  • Behavioral & Developmental Screenings
    We offer age-appropriate screenings and assessments to help identify any concerns early—because early intervention makes a difference.
  • Mental Health Support (Ages 6+)
    We provide care and resources for children struggling with anxiety, depression, attention issues, emotional regulation, or other mental health challenges. Our team offers age-appropriate screenings, parent guidance, and referrals for therapy when needed—all in a safe, supportive environment.
  • Chronic Condition Management
    Ongoing care for conditions such as asthma, ADHD, allergies, and more, with personalized plans to support your child's long-term health.
  • School & Sports Physicals
    Quick, convenient physicals to meet school, camp, or sports requirements—often available on short notice.
  • Adolescent Health
    Support for teens navigating physical, emotional, and social changes. We address topics like nutrition, mental health, sexual health, and more with respect and confidentiality.
  • Minor Procedures
    In-office procedures including ear wax removal, foreign body removal, suture removal, skin abscess drainage, wart removal with cryotherapy, and safe ear piercing for children 2+ months.
  • On-Site Laboratory Services
    Rapid testing including strep, COVID-19, Flu A&B, RSV, mono, hemoglobin, lead, urinalysis, pregnancy test, and fecal occult blood for immediate results.
  • Telemedicine Visits
    Virtual consultations available for routine follow-ups, non-emergency concerns, and convenient access to our providers from home.
  • Nutrition Counseling & Healthy Habits Program
    Comprehensive support for healthy eating habits, childhood obesity prevention, and personalized nutrition guidance for optimal growth and development.
  • Lactation Support
    Expert guidance and support for breastfeeding mothers and newborns to establish successful nursing relationships.
