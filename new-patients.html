What to Expect at Your First Visit

1

Warm Welcome

Our friendly front desk team will greet you and help you complete any remaining paperwork in our comfortable waiting area.

2

Comprehensive Exam

Your provider will conduct a thorough examination and review your child's medical history with attention to detail.

3

Care Planning

We'll discuss any concerns and create a personalized care plan for your child's ongoing health and development.

4

Portal Setup

We'll help you set up your MyPrivia patient portal account for convenient online access to records and scheduling.
