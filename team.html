Meet the dedicated physicians and nurse practitioner who lead our practice

Dr. Austin Dupont, MD

Owner & Lead Pediatrician

University of Toledo College of Medicine

As the new owner of Village Pediatrics, Dr. Dupont brings a fresh vision while honoring the foundation Dr. Elvir built. He is committed to providing comprehensive, family-centered care and building lasting relationships with families in the community.

Areas of Expertise

ADHD Diagnosis & Management
Anxiety & Depression Care
Asthma Management
Pediatric Weight Management

Dr. Patricia Elvir, MD

FAAP, ABIHM

Founding Physician

20+ Years Experience

The visionary who founded Village Pediatrics in 2008, Dr. Elvir continues to provide the compassionate care that families have trusted for years. A native New Yorker who served as Chief Resident at Schneider Children's Hospital.

Credentials & Service

Board Certified: American Academy of Pediatrics (FAAP)
Dual Certified: American Board of Integrative & Holistic Medicine (ABIHM)
Languages: Fluent in Spanish
Mission Work: Medical missions to Guatemala, Nicaragua, Haiti
Community: Ordained minister, Founder of Amigos de Juanita

Trudy English, ARNP

Advanced Registered Nurse Practitioner

Pediatric Primary Care Expert

An integral part of our care team, Trudy provides comprehensive pediatric care with expertise and compassion. She is known for her gentle, patient-centered approach and strong advocacy for preventive healthcare.

Areas of Focus

Well-child visits and developmental assessments
Pediatric sick care and acute illnesses
Family education and preventive care counseling
Immunizations and health screenings
