Our Mission

At Village Pediatrics, we aim to provide trusted, compassionate care for every child and family we serve. Our extended weekday and weekend hours are designed to meet the real-life needs and schedules of our families, ensuring quality pediatric care is always within reach. We welcome all families - including those who choose alternative or delayed vaccination schedules - with respect, open communication, and evidence-based guidance. Our mission is to build lasting relationships rooted in trust, compassion, and holistic pediatric care, helping our patients and families to grow and thrive.

Proudly serving families since 2007

Our Journey

The growth of our pediatric practice through the years

2007

The Foundation

Dr. Patricia Elvir established Village Pediatrics with a vision to serve St. Augustine families with compassionate care and medical excellence.

2007-2025

Building Trust & Excellence

For 18 years, our practice flourished! We established same-day appointments, 24/7 nursing support, and comprehensive care that families could rely upon.

September 2025

New Ownership

Dr. Austin Dupont became the new owner of Village Pediatrics, bringing fresh perspectives while preserving the established traditions and trusted care that families have grown to cherish.

What Makes Your Practice Unique?

Extended Hours for Your Convenience

We are proud to offer extended weekday hours and Saturday weekend hours designed to fit your family's schedule.

Welcoming All Families

We welcome all families, including those who follow alternative or delayed vaccine schedules, with respect and open communication.

Comprehensive, Holistic Pediatric Care

At Village Pediatrics, we champion lifelong health and wellness by providing holistic and comprehensive pediatric care - not just treating a set of symptoms. Our approach is comprehensive, addressing everything from gut health to mental health, and beyond. We believe that true pediatric care means nurturing every aspect of a child's growth, development, and well-being.

Our mission is simple - to provide accessible, personalized, and evidence-based pediatric care for every child in St. Augustine.
