A Legacy of Care Continues

Our Story

From a dream in 2008 to a trusted pediatric home - here's how Village Pediatrics grew into the practice families love today

2008 - The Beginning

Dr. Patricia Elvir, a native New Yorker and Chief Resident from Schneider Children's Hospital, founded Village Pediatrics with a vision to serve St. Augustine families with comprehensive, compassionate care.

2008-2025 - Building Trust

Over 17 years, Dr. Elvir grew Village Pediatrics into a well-loved pediatric practice, establishing same-day appointments, 24/7 after-hours nursing, and comprehensive care from newborn to age 21.

September 2025 - New Chapter

Dr. Austin Dupont becomes the new owner, bringing fresh leadership while maintaining the same compassionate care and trusted team families have come to love.
