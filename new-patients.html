Getting Started is Simple

Follow these three easy steps to become a Village Pediatrics patient

01

Call to Schedule

Contact our friendly front desk team at (904) 940-1577 to schedule your child's first appointment. We'll help you find a convenient time that works for your family.

Call Now
02

Gather Your Information

Collect your insurance cards, previous medical records, immunization records, and any medications your child is currently taking. See the complete checklist below.

View Checklist
03

Visit Our Office

Arrive 15 minutes early for your appointment to complete any remaining paperwork. Our team will welcome you and get you settled in our comfortable office.

Get Directions

What to Bring to Your First Visit

Having these items ready will help ensure a smooth first appointment

Required Documents

Current insurance cards (front and back)
Photo identification (parent/guardian)
Previous medical records (if available)
Complete immunization records

Additional Information

List of current medications
Known allergies or medical conditions
Questions or concerns to discuss
Emergency contact information

Tip: If you don't have all of these items, don't worry! We can still see your child and help you obtain missing records. Having them ready just makes the process smoother.
